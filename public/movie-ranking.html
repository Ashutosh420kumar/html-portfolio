The Best Movies According to Ashutosh

My Top 3 movies of all-time.

Tumbaad

"Tumbbad" is hailed as one of the best movies due to its unique storytelling, exceptional visuals, and thought-provoking themes. It combines horror, fantasy, and folklore to create a haunting tale that explores the consequences of unchecked greed.

Kaithi

"Kaithi" is often regarded as an exceptional movie due to its intense and gripping storyline, brilliant performances, and unique filmmaking style. The film unfolds in real-time, heightening the suspense and tension throughout. It follows the journey of a prisoner who fights against a group of ruthless criminals to save innocent lives.

Interstellar

"Interstellar" is widely regarded as one of the best movies ever made due to its visionary storytelling, breathtaking visuals, Sci-fi and thought-provoking themes.
